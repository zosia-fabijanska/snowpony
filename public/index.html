Contemporary Bar & Restaurant in Mt Buller

Snow Pony is a contemporary bar and restaurant hiding under the Timbuktu development on Bourke Street ski run.

The interior is striking and cosy, combining contemporary and traditional chalet elements in a moody mix of recycled timbers, log walls, cowhide, fire, antler chandeliers and dark marble bar.
